GOVERNMENT POLYTECHNIC GAUCHAR

THE GOVERNMENT POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE IN GAUCHAR, UTTARAKHAND WAS ESTABLISHED IS 1979. IT WAS SET UP TO PROVIDE TECHNOLOGICAL ADVANCEMENT IN THEE GARHWAL REGION.

    Departments and Courses provided:

  • Information Technology
  • Civil
  • Electronics
  • Pharmacy

    HISTORY:

  • The college was originally started with a single branch is civil engineering.
  • It is associated with the Uttarakhand Board of Technical Education in Roorkee.
  • The college offers a variety of undergraduate and after-10th level courses in various streams, inckluding engineering and medicine and health sciences.
  • The college's courses are approved by AICTE and PCL.
